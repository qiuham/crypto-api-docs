# Algo Fee Structure

## Fee Rate[​](/docs/algo/spot-algo#fee-rate "Direct link to Fee Rate")

Fees charged may vary depending on the algorithmic strategies selected. The fee schedule is as follows:

VIP Level| Fee Rate  
---|---  
Regular users and users up to VIP 4| FDUSDUSDT, USDCUSDT: 0% ;BTC/USDT, BTC/USDC, BTC/FDUSD, ETH/USDT, ETH/USDC, ETH/FDUSD: 0.05%; All Other Pairs: 0.12%  
VIP 5 and above| All listed Pairs: 0.00%
